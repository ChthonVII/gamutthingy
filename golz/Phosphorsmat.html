 Individual phosphor spectra: Phosphors.mat

The above file contains the spectral power distributions of the red (R), green (G) and blue (B) phosphors of 15 color monitors that we measured. It is a MATLAB file with 401 rows (380 nm to 780 nm, 1 nm steps), 3 columns (power of R, power of G, power of B) and 15 hypercolumns (monitor 1 to 15). Thus after loading this file into MATLAB, you get as data for monitor i (by typing "phosphors(:,:,i)"):

power of R at 380 nm power of G at 380 nm power of B at 380 nm
... ... ...
power of R at 780 nm power of G at 780 nm power of B at 780 nm

The phosphor spectra of the individual CRTs were measured separately for R, G, and B at the half-maximum intensity level and then scaled to obtain unit luminance (CIE 1931 Y = 1.0 for each spectral power distribution).
